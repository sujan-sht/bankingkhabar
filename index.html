What is Letter of Credit (LC)?

Apekshya Dhungana, Banking Khabar. Letter of Credit (LC) also known as a documentary credit is a document sent from a bank that provide guarantee to the seller (beneficiary) that they will receive a payment from buyer (applicant) timely and accurately.
